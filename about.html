About

Victor Morello is an electronic music producer, sound designer and computer enthusiast.

As an artist, he previously haunted the psychedelic trance scene under the Ivort moniker, touring worldwide and releasing numerous EPs and tracks on labels like Zenon Records, HOMmega Productions and Hadra records. He now creates experimental music as
Zero Crossing Point, aiming to create strong emotions for the listener, and blurring the lines between music and sound design.

In his spare time, he creates video games and experiments with image and video manipulation.

He has worked for 5 years as a in-house sound designer for Arturia, creating and curating thousands of factory presets, while also testing, helping define and shape several award-winning products such as Pigments, V-Collection, MatrixBrute and PolyBrute.
